Need Access?

Please review the information below to request access to the MDH data sources.

1) You are a Roche employee and need access to MDH data sources.

If this is the case, please complete the following steps:

Complete the compliance training for the data source that you would like to access:

  • Compliance Trainings are “Read and Understand” available in Roche Learning Management Systems (LSO, Chris Portal; LMS, SumTotal)
  • For a list of the compliance training LSO/LMS codes Click here for a list

Request access to MDH data source by completing the RWD web form Remember to attach the certificate of completion of the “Read and Understand” to the webform

You will receive access within three business days from your request.

2) You are not a Roche employee and need access to MDH data sources.
Note: a Third Party Agreement might be needed.

If this is the case, please review the information below:

Complete the compliance training for the data source that you would like to access.

  • Compliance Training are “Read and Understand” available in Roche Learning Management Systems (LSO, Chris Portal; LMS, SumTotal)
  • For a list of the compliance training LSO/LMS codes Click here for a list

Learn about Third Party Access (TPA) agreements:

To learn about TPA requirements by data source, please refer to the TPA Matrix

If a TPA is needed:

  • Your manager (Roche employee) will contact the Global RWD Support team global.rwdsupport@roche.com to initiate the TPA process
  • Once the TPA is signed, your manager (Roche employee) will request access on your behalf by completing the RWD web form Remember to attach the certificate of completion of the “Read and Understand” to the webform

If a TPA is not needed:

  • Your manager (Roche employee) will request access on your behalf by completing the RWD web form Remember to attach the certificate of completion of the “Read and Understand” to the webform
