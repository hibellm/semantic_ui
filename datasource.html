Clinical Practice Research Datalink

Data Source Details

Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency

The Clinical Practice Research Datalink (CPRD) is the new English NHS observational data and interventional research service, jointly funded by the NHS National Institute for Health Research (NIHR) and the Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency (MHRA). CPRD services are designed to maximize the way anonymized NHS clinical data can be linked to enable many types of observational research and deliver research outputs that are beneficial to improving and safeguarding public health.

This e-health secure research service combines the expertise of the MHRA's General Practice Research Database (GPRD) and the Department of Healthâ€™s NIHR Research Capability Programme (RCP) which piloted the service over the last four years. CPRD combines the activities of GPRD and RCP.
