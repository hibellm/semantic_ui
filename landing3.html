Purpose of the Medical Data Hub

The MDH supports Roche in its mission to be a leader using Real Word Data (RWD) and Continous Evidence Research (CER).

Why are RWD and CER important?

The development of Roche's RWD and CER capabilities help to optimize medical practice and patient benefit, accelerate and appropriately sustain patient access to therapies and innovate drug development.
