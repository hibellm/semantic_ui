Who are we?

The Medical Data Hub are:

  • A small but highly functional team with complimentary skill sets
  • Able to impliment Big Data technologies
  • Flexable and focused on delivering the analytical environment required for the future

What does the Medical Data Hub do?

The MDH supports Roche in its mission to be a leader using Real Word Data (RWD) and Continous Evidence Research (CER). RWD and CER development help to optimize medical practice and patient benefit, accelerate and appropriately sustain patient access to therapies and innovate drug development. Please click the video to hear our CEO, Severin Schwan discuss Foundation Medicine, Ownership of RWD and how we will work it in the future, and the important partnerships that we will develop to drive success.
